ABOUT US

At Whisk and Hearth, we blend passion with precision to bake memories. From our cozy kitchen in Allahabad, we craft artisanal treats that ignite smiles and satisfy cravings. Each confection at Whisk and Hearth is a labor of love, made with the finest ingredients and a dash of creativity. Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply savoring a sweet moment, our creations are here to make every occasion a little sweeter.
